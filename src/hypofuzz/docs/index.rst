Welcome to HypoFuzz!
====================

HypoFuzz is an advanced fuzzing backend and dashboard for your `Hypothesis <https://github.com/HypothesisWorks/hypothesis//>`_ tests.

Hundreds of thousands of developers trust Hypothesis to test their code, including :pypi:`NumPy <numpy>`, :pypi:`attrs`, `CPython <https://github.com/python/cpython>`_, :pypi:`pandas`, and :pypi:`cryptography`. HypoFuzz builds on top of the success of Hypothesis: HypoFuzz finds bugs quicker, gives greater insight into your test effectiveness, and makes more efficient use of CPU timeâ€”all using your same Hypothesis test suite.

We recommend starting with the :doc:`quickstart <quickstart>`. For a full reference guide, check out the :doc:`user manual <manual/index>`.
